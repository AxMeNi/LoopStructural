Advanced Use
--------------------

The Advanced Features section showcases the more sophisticated capabilities of LoopStructural. 
These examples demonstrate how to handle complex modeling scenarios, such as integrating multiple faults and folds, applying custom constraints, and fine-tuning numerical solvers. 
You'll also learn how to enhance your models with advanced control over geological parameters, boundary conditions, and geometrical relationships. 
If you're looking to push the boundaries of what LoopStructural can achieve, this section will provide the tools and techniques needed to tackle intricate geological problems with greater precision and flexibility.
